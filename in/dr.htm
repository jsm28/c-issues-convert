WG14 Defect Report Summary

The list that follows provides a brief summary of all issues raised as separate questions within Defect Reports #001 through #178. Please note that the one-sentence summaries that follow seldom do justice to the issues, which are often subtle or complex. Read them to get a sense of the area of the C Standard requiring interpretation or correction. Be warned that they may well fail to properly characterize the precise concern.
